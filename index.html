Clear answers

Built to remove friction from public contracting.

What does National Corporate Contract Exchange do?

It brings together open state and local government opportunities, compares them against a business capability profile, ranks the strongest matches, and provides deeper opportunity intelligence to support informed pursuit decisions.

Which public entities are included?

The acquisition mission includes states, counties, cities, municipalities, school districts, universities, authorities, and other public procurement publishers nationwide.

What is the Apropos Opportunity Intelligence Engine?

It is the intelligence layer that compares procurement requirements with business services, products, terminology, geography, qualifications, licenses, certifications, capacity, and other fit signals to produce ranked, explainable matches.

What is an Executive Opportunity Assessment?

It is a detailed report designed to help a business understand a selected opportunity, including the public entity, location, closing date, contact information, contract requirements, capability alignment, and relevant pursuit considerations.

What does access cost?

National Corporate Contract Exchange discovery, matching, and Executive Opportunity Assessment services are provided free of charge by Apropos Group LLC.
